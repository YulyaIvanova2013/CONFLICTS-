Conflict with Friends: Speaking Task

You are going to give a talk about conflicts with your friends. You will have to start in 1.5 minutes and speak for no more than 2 minutes (10-12 sentences).

Task Instructions

In this task, you will talk about conflicts with your friends. You need to focus on the following aspects:

  • Why teenagers can have a conflict with their friends.
  • What you usually have conflicts with your friends about.
  • How you usually make up with your friends.
  • What your attitude to conflicts with your friends is.
Prepare to speak!
